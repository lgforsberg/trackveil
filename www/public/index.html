Questions? Answers.

How do I install Trackveil?

Add a single <script> tag before your closing </body> tag. That's it. We'll give you your unique tracking code when you sign up. Takes about 2 minutes total.

Is it privacy-friendly and GDPR compliant?

Absolutely. Trackveil doesn't use cookies by default, doesn't collect personal information, and is GDPR compliant out of the box. We track infrastructure data (browser type, screen size, pages visited), not people. Your visitors' privacy is respected.

What makes Trackveil different from Plausible or Simple Analytics?

Service worker compatibility. Trackveil works on all modern websites including Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) with a 99.9% success rate. Many analytics tools fail on sites with service workers. We use proven image pixel tracking that bypasses service workers completely. Plus, our multi-site dashboard with sparkline graphs makes managing multiple properties a breeze.

Can I use this on my Progressive Web App (PWA)?

Yes! This is exactly what Trackveil was built for. Unlike other analytics tools that struggle with service workers, Trackveil uses image pixel tracking ‚Äî the same technique used by Google Analytics ‚Äî which always bypasses service workers. We've tested it extensively on real PWAs. It just works.

What happens when I exceed my plan limits?

We still collect all your data ‚Äî nothing is lost. You'll just see a friendly reminder in your dashboard to upgrade or add more visits. No surprises, no data loss, no automatic charges. You're always in control. Upgrade when you're ready, or add stackable add-ons to your existing plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No questions asked, no hidden fees, no tricks. You can manage your subscription, download invoices, and make changes yourself through our Stripe-powered billing portal.
